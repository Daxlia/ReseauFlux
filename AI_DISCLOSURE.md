## Transparency Declaration

In accordance with the principles of the European Code of Conduct for Research Integrity, which mandate that the use of AI or automated tools in the creation of content or the writing of publications must not be concealed, this document provides a complete and transparent disclosure of how Large Language Models (LLMs) have been utilized in the development of this project.

The AI systems were employed as assistive tools to support, enhance, and accelerate the research and development process. All AI-generated outputs have been rigorously reviewed, tested, and validated by the author, who assumes full responsibility for the final content and its licensing.

## Specific AI Tool Usage

The following tools were used for distinct phases of the project, as detailed below:

### 1. Code Generation – Claude (Anthropic)
- **Tool:** Claude (Pro Plan)
- **Primary Role:** Development of core Python code, including the main pipeline script (`metabolic_ml_pipeline.py`), algorithm implementation (e.g., Gradient Boosting, model ranking logic, FBA handling), and structuring of the analysis.
- **Scope:** A substantial portion of the code structure and logic was generated with the assistance of Claude. All generated code underwent thorough review, manual debugging, and adaptation to ensure correctness, safety, and adherence to project requirements.

### 2. Code Review, Debugging & Suggestions – DeepSeek & Grok (xAI)
- **Tools:** DeepSeek, Grok (xAI)
- **Primary Role:** Code review, error identification, and suggestion of improvements.
- **Scope:** These tools were employed to assist in debugging sessions, identify potential issues in the code, and propose alternative solutions or improvements. The suggestions were critically evaluated before being implemented by the author.

### 3. Research, Reflection, and Exploration – ChatGPT (OpenAI)
- **Tool:** ChatGPT
- **Primary Role:** Literature exploration, brainstorming of methodologies, and high-level design considerations.
- **Scope:** ChatGPT was used as a research assistant to support the initial phases of the project, helping to frame problems, explore background information, and reflect on architectural choices. No generative output was directly incorporated without significant review and adaptation.

## Author's Responsibility

The author hereby confirms that:

- The final work reflects the author's own understanding, decisions, and research contributions.
- The use of AI has been disclosed in accordance with the highest standards of research integrity and transparency.
